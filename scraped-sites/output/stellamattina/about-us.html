About Stella Mattina

Compassionate women's health and family medicine — serving Dallas and the DFW metroplex with personalized care.

Stella Mattina is a Dallas-based women's health and family medicine practice committed to providing personalized, compassionate care for patients at every stage of life. With more than 29 expert providers across 7 DFW locations, we combine clinical excellence with a warm, patient-centered approach.

Our Mission

We empower our patients to understand their choices and take control of their health. From fertility consultations and birth control guidance to prenatal care, deliveries, and post-partum support, our experienced professionals provide compassionate and personalized care and advice.

Comprehensive Women's Health

At Stella Mattina, we understand the unique health needs of women in their productive years and beyond. Our dedicated OBGYN providers offer specialized care for every stage of your life:

  • Annual exams and preventive screenings
  • Gynecology and minimally invasive surgery
  • Prenatal care and delivery services
  • Maternal-fetal medicine for high-risk pregnancies
  • Hormone therapy (BioTE pellet therapy)
  • Fertility consultations
  • Birth control counseling and IUD placement
  • Postpartum care and support

Family Medicine for All Ages

Our family medicine practices in Dallas and Carrollton provide comprehensive primary care for patients of all ages — from pediatrics through senior care. Preventive wellness, chronic disease management, and acute care are all part of what we offer.
