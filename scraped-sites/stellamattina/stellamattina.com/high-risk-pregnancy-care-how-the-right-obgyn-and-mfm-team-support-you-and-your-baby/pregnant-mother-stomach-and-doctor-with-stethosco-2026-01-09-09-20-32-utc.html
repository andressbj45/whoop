Why Choose Us?

At Stella Mattina, your convenience, accessibility, and well-being are our top priorities. So, here’s why we stand out as your premier healthcare choice.

1. Same-Day Appointments

Your health matters, and we understand the urgency of your needs. With same-day appointments available, you can receive timely care when you need it most.

2. Walk-ins Welcome

We welcome walk-ins because we believe in making healthcare accessible to all. Whether you have an urgent concern or need immediate attention, our doors are always open.

3. Fast Response

Your time is valuable, and we’re committed to providing swift responses to your healthcare needs. Experience efficient and effective care with our responsive team.

4. BioTe Providers Available

We’re proud to offer BioTe hormone therapy, empowering you to optimize your health and vitality. Trust our experienced BioTe providers for personalized hormone optimization solutions.

5. Many Convenient Locations

With many conveniently located centers across the Dallas-Fort Worth area, accessing quality healthcare has never been easier. Find a location near you and experience the Stella Mattina difference.

6. Accepted Insurances

We understand the importance of affordability in healthcare. That’s why we accept most insurances, to make certain you have access to comprehensive care without financial barriers.

7. Medicare and Medicaid Accepted

You should never compromise your health due to insurance limitations. Stella Mattina proudly accepts Medicare and Medicaid, providing inclusive care to all members of our community.

8. Self-Pay Options Available

For those without insurance coverage or who prefer self-pay options, we offer flexible payment solutions. You’re in good hands with Stella Mattina, regardless of your insurance status.

Looking for the best OBGYN in Dallas and excellent primary care? We offer same-day appointments and use a comprehensive list of insurance providers. Choose your nearest location and discover the difference we make to women’s and family health.
